Every day, you can get a little bit better at using your social networks.

No matter what your goals are on Twitter, Facebook or Google+, ThinkUp can show you the progress you're making.

Search and control your social network data

It's your stuff — you should be able to find your friends and activity. Want to search your followers' bios, or find that old tweet? You're all set.

The next time a site or app creeps you out, you'll be ready.

ThinkUp gives you a copy of your social networking activity that you control, no matter how they change their terms of service.
